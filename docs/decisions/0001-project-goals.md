# 0001 – Project Goals and Non-Goals

## Status
Proposed

## Context

Kartoush is in an early, pre-implementation phase. Before introducing
architecture, tooling, or production code, the project needs a clear
statement of intent and boundaries.

Without explicit goals and non-goals, future decisions risk being driven
by convenience or familiarity rather than purpose.

This decision establishes the guiding objectives for the project and
defines what is intentionally out of scope.

---

## Goals

- Establish a clear problem space that Kartoush is intended to explore
- Favor intentional design and documented decisions over rapid implementation
- Create a foundation that supports maintainability and long-term ownership
- Treat documentation and decision records as first-class artifacts
- Enable learning and exploration without committing prematurely to solutions
